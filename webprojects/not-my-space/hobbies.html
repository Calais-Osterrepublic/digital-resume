Some More Fun Facts about Me:

  • My favorite color is red, and I typically prefer the warm color spectrum.
  • I live with two cats. Cats > Dogs, by the way.
  • I converted my home laptop from Windows into Linux Mint.
  • Out of all of the Filipino dishes I've had, my three favorites are:
    1. Adobo: Apparently, it was traditionally an Iberian (Spanish/Portugese) dish. Neat!
    2. Pancit: A common term for many noodle dishes in the Philippines, my mom typically made them with rice noodles.
    3. Sabaw: Pronounced by me as "suh-bao" and translating directly into "soup," Sabaw was typically prepared by my mom with spinach and lettuce, giving me a taste for leafy greens, and ginger.
  • I used to dabble in clay making at some point (a fruitless endeavor).
