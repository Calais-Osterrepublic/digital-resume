How to make: Dry Oatmeal

You will need:

  • Peanut Butter, or any nut butter you prefer.
  • Oatmeal
  • Milk or Water
  • Necessary non-food utilities (spoon, bowl, microwave,etc.)

Instructions:

  1. Pour the oatmeal into a bowl
  2. Pour the milk/water into the bowl. Pour between enough to wet the oats and enough to just submerge the oats. If something is floating, you put too much.
  3. Mix in the peanut butter(roughly a spoonful)
  4. Place the mixture in the microwave for 1 minute.
  5. Let cool or place in container and cool for later use. Don't leave in a cheap lunch bag overnight.
